# Supplier Cost Analysis 2024

**Supplier:** Reliable Materials Inc.
**Period:** January - September 2024
**Analysis Date:** October 2024

## Executive Summary

Reliable Materials Inc. provides competitive pricing with good service levels. Total cost of ownership is favorable due to quality and delivery consistency. Recommend continuing and expanding relationship with focus on sustainability and digital integration.

## Cost Structure Analysis

### Direct Costs

- Material costs: $672,000
- Shipping costs: $24,000 (3.6% of material cost)
- Expedite fees: $3,500
- **Total direct costs: $699,500**

### Indirect Costs

- Quality inspection labor: $8,400
- Material rejections and rework: $8,000
- Inventory carrying costs: $28,000
- Procurement labor: $15,000
- **Total indirect costs: $59,400**

### Total Cost of Ownership: $758,900

### TCO as % of material cost: 112.9%

## Cost Savings Opportunities

### Identified Opportunities

1. **VMI Implementation**
   - Potential inventory reduction: 30%
   - Carrying cost savings: $8,400/year
   - Reduced emergency orders: $7,000/year

2. **Material Optimization**
   - Alternative materials for non-critical uses: $25,000/year
   - Waste reduction through better packaging: $5,000/year

3. **Process Improvements**
   - Digital ordering (reduce labor): $6,000/year
   - Automated quality checks: $4,000/year

4. **Volume Leverage**
   - Projected 2025 volume qualifies for 8% discount
   - Additional savings: $30,000/year

**Total potential savings: $85,400/year**
