# Materials Supply Contract 2024

**Effective Date:** January 15, 2024
**Agreement Between:** Our Company and Reliable Materials Inc.

## Terms and Conditions

1. **Products Supplied:** Raw materials and packaging supplies
2. **Contract Duration:** 36 months
3. **Payment Terms:** Net 60 days
4. **Minimum Order Value:** $75,000/quarter

## Product Categories

- Industrial grade plastics
- Metals and alloys
- Packaging materials
- Specialty chemicals

## Pricing Structure

- Base pricing with quarterly adjustments
- Volume discounts: 7% for orders > $50,000
- Annual volume rebate: Up to 3% based on total spend
- Price protection: 90-day notice for increases

## Delivery and Logistics

- Standard delivery: 14 business days
- Expedited delivery: 5 business days (+25% surcharge)
- Free shipping on orders > $10,000
- Drop-shipping available to customer locations

## Quality and Certifications

- All materials certified to industry standards
- Material certificates provided with each shipment
- Third-party testing available
- Traceability documentation included
