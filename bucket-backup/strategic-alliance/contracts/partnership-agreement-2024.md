# Strategic Partnership Agreement 2024

**Effective Date:** May 1, 2024
**Agreement Between:** Our Company and Strategic Alliance Partners

## Partnership Objectives

1. **Joint Go-to-Market:** Collaborate on enterprise sales opportunities
2. **Technology Integration:** Deep product integration between platforms
3. **Co-Marketing:** Joint marketing campaigns and events
4. **Mutual Referrals:** Revenue sharing on qualified referrals

## Terms and Conditions

- **Contract Duration:** 36 months
- **Exclusivity:** Non-exclusive partnership
- **Revenue Sharing:** 20% commission on referred deals
- **Minimum Commitment:** 5 qualified referrals per quarter

## Joint Offerings

### Integrated Solution Package

- Combined platform offering
- Single sign-on integration
- Unified customer support
- Joint implementation services

### Target Markets

- Enterprise manufacturing
- Healthcare organizations
- Financial services
- Professional services firms

## Marketing and Sales

### Co-Marketing Commitments

- 2 joint webinars per quarter
- Quarterly industry events
- Case study development
- Joint content marketing

### Sales Collaboration

- Shared lead pipeline
- Joint sales calls on enterprise deals
- Coordinated account planning
- Quarterly sales training

## Financial Terms

- Referral fees paid within 45 days of deal closure
- Quarterly reconciliation of referred revenue
- Annual volume bonuses for exceeding targets
